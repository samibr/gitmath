We apply the mean value theorem to the function $t\mapsto \ln t$ on the interval $[x,x+1]$. It exists $c\in (x,x+1)$ such that
$$ \ln(1+x)-\ln x= \frac1 c.$$

Since $x<c< x+1$, we have

$$\frac1{x+1}<\frac1 c<\frac1 x,$$

and we get the desired result.
